## Why Pywel Demands This Power
* **Proprietary BlackSpace Engine:** Features GPU-based cloth and hair simulations that require modern Tensor cores to render fluidly during combat.
* **Massive Asset Streaming:** With a map larger than *Red Dead 2*, the game requires high-speed PCIe Gen4 or Gen5 SSDs to prevent "invisible walls" while traveling at high speeds.
* **Advanced Fluid Simulations:** Real-time water ripples and volumetric fog use "Froxel Raymarching," a heavy GPU task that defines the game's atmosphere.
