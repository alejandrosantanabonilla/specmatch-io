## Why You Need This Power
* **Complex Multi-Operator Battles:** Simultaneously managing multiple operators and real-time skill effects crushes older quad-core CPUs.
* **High VRAM Demands:** 4GB GPUs face significant texture pop-in during cinematic cutscenes; 6GB+ is now the absolute baseline.
* **SSD Speed is Non-Negotiable:** Transitioning between your base and the open world can take over 30 seconds on an HDD; an NVMe SSD is required for seamless play.
