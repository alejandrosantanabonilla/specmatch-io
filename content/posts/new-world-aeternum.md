## Why You Need This Power
*   **Massive Open World:** Aeternum is sprawling. It's packed with detail, dense foliage, and dynamic weather. This isn't a small map. Your GPU renders *everything*.
*   **Player-Heavy Zones:** MMORPGs are CPU hungry. Think bustling towns, large-scale PvP, and boss raids. Your CPU has to manage hundreds of player actions, NPCs, and physics interactions. It's a bottleneck waiting to happen.
*   **Lumberyard's Demands:** The Amazon Games engine, derived from CryEngine, is known for its visual fidelity but also its system demands. Expect high draw calls and a need for strong single-core CPU performance.
